Item 7.01. Regulation FD Disclosure.

On February 17, 2022, DICE Therapeutics, Inc. (the “Company”) intends to present an updated corporate presentation at the 11th Annual SVB Leerink Global Healthcare Conference held in New York, New York or via virtual format. In connection with such presentation, the Company will announce the nomination of its new therapeutic candidate, DC-853, a fast-follower compound in the Company’s oral IL-17 franchise. A copy of the corporate presentation is attached as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K. The corporate presentation will also be available on the Company’s website in the Investors section at https://investors.dicetherapeutics.com.

The information furnished with this report, including Exhibit 99.1, shall not be deemed “filed” for purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (“Exchange Act”), or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section, nor shall it be deemed incorporated by reference into any other filing under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, or the Exchange Act, except as expressly set forth by specific reference in such a filing.
