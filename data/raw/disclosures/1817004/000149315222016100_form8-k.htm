Item 5.02 Departure of Directors or Certain Officers; Election of Directors; Appointment of Certain Officers; Compensatory Arrangements of Certain Officers.

 

As noted in Item 5.07 below, on June 3, 2022, the stockholders of EzFill Holdings, Inc. (the “Company”) approved the Company’s EzFill Holdings, Inc. 2022 Equity Incentive Plan (the “2022 Plan”), pursuant to which 2,600,000 shares of the Company’s common stock will be made available for issuance under the 2022 Plan. The 2022 Plan is described in more detail in the Company’s 2022 Proxy Statement, which was filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on April 25, 2022. A copy of the 2022 Plan is attached hereto as Exhibit 10.1.

 

On June 3, 2022, the Board of Directors of the Company (the “Board”) approved the issuance of shares of common stock to non-employee members of the Board pursuant to the Company’s EzFill Holdings, Inc. 2020 Equity Incentive Plan and 2022 Plan in connection with their appointments to the Board in the aggregate amount of 776,762 shares (the “Shares”). The Shares will fully vest in 12 months from the grant date or one day before the following year’s annual meeting, whichever is sooner. Should a member leave the Board prior to vesting, that member will be awarded a grant on a pro rata basis based on the time spent as a member of the Board.
