Item 8.01 Other Events.

 

On November 16, 2021, the Company issued a press release announcing the launch of its Gaps in Care service helping health plan members stay current with annual exams, vaccines, and health screenings. The effort is an expansion of Marpai’s premium, AI-powered services and automatically sends personalized texts and emails to remind members when it is time to make an appointment. A copy of the press release is furnished with this Current Report as Exhibit 99.1 hereto and is incorporated herein by reference
