Item 8.01    Other Events.

The date for expiration of the lockup period in connection with Remitly’s initial public offering (the "IPO") of common stock is now confirmed. All shares of equity subject to such lockup will become eligible to trade at market open on March 4, 2022.

The Company estimates that approximately 163.5 million shares of common stock will become eligible for sale in the public market at the open of trading on March 4, 2022, which includes shares of common stock issued prior to the IPO and shares issuable upon exercise of options or RSUs that will vest on or prior to March 4, 2022. This includes shares held by affiliates which are subject to volume and other restrictions of Rule 144 of the Securities Act of 1933, as amended.
