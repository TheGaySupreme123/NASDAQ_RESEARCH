Item 2.02. Results of Operations and Financial Condition.

 

On March 14, 2022, Thorne HealthTech, Inc. (the "Company") issued a press release announcing the results of the Company’s operations for the quarter and year ended December 31, 2021. The full text of the press release is furnished as Exhibit 99.1 to this Current Report on Form 8-K and is incorporated herein by reference. The Company intends to make certain supplemental information available on its website www.thorne.com under the section “Investors — Events & Presentations” prior to the Company’s conference call with investors on Tuesday, March 15, 2022 at 10:00 a.m. (Eastern Time).
