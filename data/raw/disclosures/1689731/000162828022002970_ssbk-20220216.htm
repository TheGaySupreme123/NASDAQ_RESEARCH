Item 8.01.    Other Events.

On February 16, 2022, Southern States Bancshares, Inc. (the "Company") announced that its board of directors has approved a stock repurchase program (the "Program") that authorizes the Company to repurchase up to $10 million of its common stock. The Program will be in effect until December 31, 2022 with the timing of purchases and number of shares repurchased under the Program dependent upon a variety of factors including price, trading volume, corporate and regulatory requirements, and market conditions. The Company is not obligated to purchase any shares under the Program and the Program may be suspended or discontinued at any time without notice. A copy of the press release issued by the Company announcing the Program is attached to this report as Exhibit 99.1 and is incorporated herein.
