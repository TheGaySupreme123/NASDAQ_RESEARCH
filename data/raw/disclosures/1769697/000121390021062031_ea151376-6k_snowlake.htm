Issuance of Press Release

 

On November 24, 2021, Snow Lake Resources Ltd. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing the closing of its initial public offering of 3,680,000 common shares, no par value per share (the “Common Shares”), including 480,000 Common Shares sold upon full exercise of the underwriters’ over-allotment option, at a price to the public of $7.50 per Common Share, before deducting underwriting discounts, commissions and offering expenses.

 

The press release furnished in this report as Exhibit 99.1 shall not be deemed to be “filed” for the purposes of Section 18 of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934 or otherwise subject to the liabilities of that section.
