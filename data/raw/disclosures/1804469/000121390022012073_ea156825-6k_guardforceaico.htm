Entry into a Sale and Purchase Agreement

 

On March 11, 2022, Guardforce AI Co., Limited (the “Company”) entered into a Sale and Purchase Agreement (the “Agreement”) with Shenzhen Kewei Robot Technology Co., Limited (“Shenzhen Kewei”) relating to the previously proposed acquisition of Shenzhen Keweien Robot Service Co., Ltd. and Guangzhou Kewei Robot Technology Co., Ltd. from Shenzhen Kewei. This acquisition is expected to serve an integral role in the growth of Guardforce AI’s robotics as a service (RaaS) business initiative. This acquisition is expected to be closed by end of April 2022.

 

A press release was issued on March 11, 2022 and is attached as Exhibit 99.1 hereto.
