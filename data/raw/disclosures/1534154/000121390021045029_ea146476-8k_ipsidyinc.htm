Item 8.01. Other Events.

 

On August 26, 2021, Ipsidy Inc. (the “Company”) issued a press release announcing the closing of the Company’s previously announced public offering (the “Offering”) of 1,642,856 shares of its common stock at a public offering price of $7.00 per share, including 214,285 shares sold upon full exercise of the underwriter’s option to purchase additional shares, for gross proceeds of approximately $11.5 million, before deducting underwriting discounts and offering expenses.
