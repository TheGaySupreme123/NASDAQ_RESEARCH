<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>Item 1. Description of Registrant&rsquo;s Securities to be Registered.</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">The securities to be registered hereby are the units,
Class&nbsp;A ordinary shares, par value $0.0001 per share (&ldquo;Class A ordinary shares&rdquo;), rights, and redeemable warrants
of Jaguar Global Growth Corporation I (the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;). Each right entitles the holder thereof to receive one twelfth (1/12) of one Class A ordinary share, and each whole redeemable warrant
is exercisable for one Class A ordinary share. The description of the units, Class&nbsp;A ordinary shares,
rights and redeemable warrants contained in the section entitled &ldquo;Description of Securities&rdquo; in the prospectus included
in the Company&rsquo;s Registration Statement on Form&nbsp;S-1 (File No.&nbsp;333-260483), originally filed with the Securities and
Exchange Commission on October&nbsp;25, 2021, as thereafter amended and supplemented from time to time (the &ldquo;Registration
Statement&rdquo;), to which this Form&nbsp;8-A relates, is incorporated herein by reference. Any form of prospectus or prospectus
supplement to the Registration Statement that includes such descriptions and that is subsequently filed is also incorporated by
reference herein.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>
