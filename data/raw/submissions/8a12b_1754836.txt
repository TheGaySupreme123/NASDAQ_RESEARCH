Registered.</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">The securities to be registered hereby are shares of 5.00% Series A
Term Preferred Stock due 2026 (the &#8220;<U>Shares</U>&#8221;) of Eagle Point Income Company Inc., a Delaware corporation (the &#8220;<U>Registrant</U>&#8221;).
The Shares are expected to be listed on the New York Stock Exchange and to trade thereon within 30 days of the original issue date under
the trading symbol &#8220;EICA.&#8221;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">A description of Shares is set forth under the heading &#8220;The Offering,&#8221;
 &#8220;Description of Our Capital Stock&#8221; and &#8220;Description of the Series A Term Preferred Stock&#8221; in the Registrant&#8217;s
prospectus included in the Registrant&#8217;s registration statement on Form&nbsp;N-2 (as amended from time to time, the &#8220;<U>Registration
Statement</U>&#8221;), which Registration Statement was initially filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission (the &#8220;<U>Commission</U>&#8221;)
on August 23, 2021 under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended (the &#8220;<U>Securities Act</U>&#8221;), and the Investment Company
Act of 1940, as amended (File Nos. 333-259029 and 811-23384, respectively), and in the prospectus, dated October 18, 2021 (the &#8220;
<U>Prospectus</U>&#8221;), as filed with the Commission pursuant to Rule 424 under the Securities Act on October 19, 2021. The descriptions
of the Shares contained in the Prospectus and in any form of prospectus or prospectus supplement to the Registration Statement that includes
such descriptions and that are subsequently filed with the Commission under the Securities Act, are hereby incorporated by reference herein.</P>
