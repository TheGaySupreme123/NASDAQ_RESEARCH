<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>Item 1. Description of Registrant&rsquo;s Securities to Be Registered.</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">The securities to be registered hereby are the units, Class&nbsp;A
ordinary shares, par value $0.0001 per share, and redeemable warrants, with each whole warrant exercisable for one Class&nbsp;A ordinary
share of SilverBox Corp IV (the &ldquo;Company&rdquo;). The description of the units, Class&nbsp;A ordinary shares and redeemable warrants
contained in the section entitled &ldquo;Description of Securities&rdquo; in the prospectus included in the Company&rsquo;s Registration
Statement on Form&nbsp;S-1 (File No.&nbsp;333-280315), originally filed with the Securities and Exchange Commission on June&nbsp;18,
2024, as thereafter amended and supplemented from time to time (the &ldquo;Registration Statement&rdquo;), to which this Form&nbsp;8-A
relates, is incorporated herein by reference. Any form of prospectus or prospectus supplement to the Registration Statement that includes
such descriptions and that is subsequently filed is also incorporated by reference herein.</P>
