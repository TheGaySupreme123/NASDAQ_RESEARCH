<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-align: center"><B>Explanatory Note</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">Ramaco Resources,&nbsp;Inc. (the &ldquo;Company&rdquo; or the &ldquo;Registrant&rdquo;)
is filing this registration statement on Form&nbsp;8-A with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (the &ldquo;SEC&rdquo;), in connection
with the registration pursuant to Section&nbsp;12(b)&nbsp;of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended, of its Class&nbsp;B common
stock, par value $0.01 per share (&ldquo;Class&nbsp;B common stock&rdquo;), in connection with the distribution by the Company to holders
of existing common stock, par value $0.01 (the &ldquo;existing common stock&rdquo;), of shares of Class&nbsp;B common stock.</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">The description of the Class&nbsp;B common stock is set forth under
the heading &ldquo;Description of Common Stock&rdquo; in the Registrant&rsquo;s prospectus forming part of its Registration Statement
on Form&nbsp;S-1 (File No.&nbsp;333-267152), originally filed with the SEC on August&nbsp;30, 2022, as thereafter amended and supplemented
from time to time (the &ldquo;Registration Statement&rdquo;) to which this Form&nbsp;8-A relates, and which is incorporated by reference
herein. In addition, all of the above-referenced descriptions included in any prospectus relating to the Registration Statement filed
with the Commission pursuant to Rule&nbsp;424(b)&nbsp;under the Securities Act of 1933, as amended, shall be deemed to be incorporated
by reference herein.</P>
