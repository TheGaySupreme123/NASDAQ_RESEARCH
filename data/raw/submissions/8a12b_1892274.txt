<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0"><B>Item 1. Description of Registrant&rsquo;s Securities to be Registered.</B></P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0">&nbsp;</P>

<P STYLE="font: 10pt Times New Roman, Times, Serif; margin: 0pt 0; text-indent: 0.5in">Visionary Education Technology Holdings Group Inc.
(the &ldquo;Registrant&rdquo;) hereby registers its common shares, no par value (&ldquo;Common Shares&rdquo;) pursuant to Section 12(b)
of the Securities Exchange Act of 1934, as amended (the &ldquo;Exchange Act&rdquo;). The description of the Common Shares contained in
the sections entitled &ldquo;Description of Share Capital&rdquo; in the Registrant's prospectus forming part of its Registration Statement
on Form F-1 (File No. 333-263290), as initially filed publicly with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (the &ldquo;Commission&rdquo;)
on March 4, 2022, and as thereafter amended, to which this Form 8-A relates, is incorporated by herein by reference. Copies of such description
will be filed with The Nasdaq Stock Market LLC.</P>
